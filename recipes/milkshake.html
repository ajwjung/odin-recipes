Vanilla Milkshake

Description

A vanilla milkshake is always a great choice when you want a sweet drink. Milkshakes pair are also very kid-friendly. Try this super simple and quick recipe to satisfy that craving!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Blend ice cream, milk, and vanilla extract together in a blender until smooth; pour into glasses and serve.
