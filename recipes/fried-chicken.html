Triple-Dipped Fried Chicken

Description

Fried chicken is a must have while watching games or just when you're craving some nice crispy chicken. No need to order takeout when you can follow this simple recipe for some mouthwatering fried chicken!

Ingredients

  1. 1 quart vegetable oil for frying
  2. 4 1/3 cups all-purpose flour, divided
  3. 1 1/2 teaspoons garlic salt
  4. 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
  5. 1 tablespoon paprika
  6. 1/2 teaspoon poultry seasoning
  7. 1 1/2 cups beer, or as needed
  8. 2 egg yolks, beaten
  9. 1 teaspoon salt
  10. 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  11. 1 (3 pound) whole chicken, cut into pieces
