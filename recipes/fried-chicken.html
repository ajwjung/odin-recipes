Triple-Dipped Fried Chicken

Description

Fried chicken is a must have while watching games or just when you're craving some nice crispy chicken. No need to order takeout when you can follow this simple recipe for some mouthwatering fried chicken!

Ingredients

  • 1 quart vegetable oil for frying
  • 4 1/3 cups all-purpose flour, divided
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons garlic salt
  • 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon poultry seasoning
  • 1 1/2 cups beer, or as needed
  • 2 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (3 pound) whole chicken, cut into pieces

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a deep-fryer to 350 degres F (175 degrees C)
  2. Mix together 3 cups flour, garlic salt, 1 tablespoon pepper, paprika, and poultry seasoning in a medium bowl.
  3. Stir together 1 1/2 cups beer, remaining 1 1/3 cups flour, egg yolks, salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper in a separate bowl; thin mixture with more beer if batter is too thick.
  4. Moisten chicken pieces with a bit of water, then dip in the dry mixture. Shake off excess and dip in the wet mixture, then dip in the dry mixture once more.
  5. Fry chicken pieces in hot oil until well-browned and itnernal temperature reaches 165 degrees F (74 degrees C), 15 to 18 minutes.
  6. Remove chicken pieces and drain on paper towels before serving.
